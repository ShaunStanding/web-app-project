About us

bits.com is an industry leader in selling new, second hand and refurbished retro consoles and accessories. The team have a combined 180
years of experience within the retro console industry.
The expertise we have includes game developers who worked on famous titles, such as Zelda, Donkey Kong and Missile Command.
We also host a range of in house hardware experts, some of whom helped create the Sega Genesis and the Commodore 64!
